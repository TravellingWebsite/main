Introduction
This route is designed to encourage people to pay attention to and understand the beautiful scenery around the main campus of Tohoku University, and it is a Sendai bus route that is perfect for bringing your family and friends to experience the rich culture. Many people think that these places are common and there is always a chance to go, but in the end, they often don't have time to go until graduation, so take this opportunity to meet up with friends and family, and slowly follow the bus along to experience the natural river, greenery, and Japanese culture.

1. あおば通駅+青葉通一番町駅

经过青叶通，有很多宠物咖啡和arcade。别的我不知道留给都市丽人补充

2. 東北大正門前：片平学都記念公園+東北大学史料館
(In front of the Tohoku University Gate: Katahira Gakuto Memorial Park + Tohoku University Historical Museum)

Tohoku University's natural scenery is unique among Japanese universities, especially the park during the cherry blossom season, which is very dreamy, attracting nearby residents to come here for recreation. In other seasons, there is also abundant greenery, and there are many historic 100-year-old trees. In addition, at the beginning of the semester, the Senate often holds tours, which basically cover all the attractions of the Katahira campus, including the Historical Museum and the statue of Lu Xun, so if you are interested, you can search the keyword "Katahira Campus Tour" in the google.

3. 霊屋橋・瑞鳳殿入口（広瀬川）：アルブマロカフェ・ＴＬＹ
Reiya Bridge / Zuihoden Entrance (Hirose River): Albumaro Cafe / TLY

Hop off at this stop and meet the tour plan of food first and history second. This coffee shop is very exotic and loved by foreign residents. Not only do we not overadvertise local products, but we also prepare some overseas treasures. The warm décor, the knowledgeable owner, and the wide variety of dishes make it an option for afternoon tea or lunch. After drinking coffee, you will visit the Ruifeng Hall and the Wuchen Soul Monument slowly, and you will definitely have an endless aftertaste.

4. 八木山入口長徳寺前：吉田屋食堂+長徳寺
Yagiyama entrance Chotokuji Temple: Yoshidaya Shokudo + Chotokuji Temple

If you've already been to Ruifeng Hall and it's close to lunchtime, you can choose to get off here and spend a good noon. This restaurant is a great value for money, with a wide variety of inexpensive dishes, including family-friendly rice bowls, fried rice, and ramen, so it's a great place to stop and feel the Japanese set meal culture during your trip. After the meal, you can relax and feel the fresh air and a moment of tranquility at the Buddhist temple Chotokuji Temple overlooking the city in the distance.

5. 八木山神社前（香澄町）（3/4/5重复度高- -都是吃的）：
In front of Yagiyama Shrine (Kasumi Town)

1) Mainly recommended stores.
(1) スパイスエイト: Exotic shops opened in residential areas must have their own characteristics to last for a long time. Sendai's spice curries are among the top hidden shops, so don't miss it if you like to collect flavored curries.
(2) いずみや珈琲豆専門店: An exquisite independent shop in a single-family house, selling coffee beans and handmade desserts, with a gentle view outside, suitable for taking photos and coffee bean lovers, and friendly to beginners.

6. 八木山ベニーランド前+八木山動物公園駅+とんかつ大町 八木山店
Yagiyama Benny Land Mae + Yagiyama Zoological Park Station + Tonkatsu Omachi Yagiyama Store

Yagiyama Zoological Park is a familiar presence to every passenger who takes the East-West Subway Line, but most people rarely really take the time to explore this area of Yagiyama. In addition to the Showa-style amusement park and zoo located next to each other, it is perfect for taking pictures. In addition, the amusement park has been very popular since the last century, and as the only amusement park in the Sendai area, a visit to the amusement park is sure to bring you closer to the locals and learn more about Sendai.

You can also enjoy tonkatsu before and after your visit, and although it is a chain store, it is highly rated all over Sendai. The price of the special Marshal Tonkatsu is slightly higher, but the reviews are very good. If you like to try it, you can choose here, and it is recommended to come and play after eating at the first two stops.
